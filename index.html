ABOUT MOHIT

Mohit Bhoite is an award-winning Director of Photography based in Vadodara, India. With over 2 years of experience in the film industry, Mohit has developed a distinctive visual style that combines cinematic storytelling with technical precision.

His work spans feature films, commercials, music videos, and documentary projects. Mohit's ability to capture emotion through light and composition has made him one of the most sought-after cinematographers in the industry.

When not on set, Mohit enjoys mentoring young filmmakers and experimenting with new camera technologies to push the boundaries of visual storytelling.
